Our Beliefs

This is MOA
Why MOA

Precision design, attention to detail and quality delivery is the core focus of the MOA team.

What makes us unique:

  • A Choice in Cladding Options.
  • Triple Glazed Window Units.
  • The Fabric of all Studios meets or exceeds relevant sections of the building regulations.
  • Certified by Chartered Engineer.
  • Bespoke built-in furniture, unique to your studio.
  • Range of Architectural Ironmongery.

Each project is specific to you, the end user. Your requirements are the foundation of how we build. We design and manufacture studios for installation across the country from our purpose-built workshop situated in the heart of Ireland.

Our Promise
Proud & Dedicated

We are proud of our dedicated team, all of whom are committed to ensuring that every detail is taken care of. Upon completion of your project, you will be issued with certification by our chartered engineer.

MOA has moved away from traditional building techniques. Instead we have created a seamless modular building process, that is both innovative and non-invasive. The installation of your garden studio is quick and hassle-free, taking on average 12 working days to complete.

This experience is one of collaboration. We recognise that communication is the key to ensuring your final bespoke garden studio is what you want it to be. We are committed to offering the best craftsmanship available.
